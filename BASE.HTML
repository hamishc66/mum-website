The Artist

Maggie Cairns

"Painting is how I remember to breathe."

Maggie Cairns is a contemporary artist working from her studio in the Scottish Highlands. Her practice draws on the elemental rhythms of landscape, memory, and the body's quiet intelligence.

Each piece begins not with a sketch but with a feeling — a residue of light on water, the colour of grief, the particular stillness that follows rain. The work invites the viewer to slow down and inhabit that same quality of attention.

Maggie's paintings are held in private collections across Europe and Australia. All works are available for direct inquiry.
